# Play 20 Questions — Prompts

## Let's play!
Think of something and I'll try to guess it in 20 yes-or-no questions.

## I'll think of something — you guess
I'm thinking of something. You ask me yes-or-no questions and try to guess what it is in 20 questions.

## Make it really hard
Think of something tricky — I want a real challenge this time.

## Pick a category
Let me choose a category (animal, place, food, movie, etc.) and then think of something in that category.

## Play again
That was fun — let's play another round!
