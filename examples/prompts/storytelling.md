# Storytelling & Characters

## Character Creator
Come up with three characters. Give them names, physical descriptions and a two sentence backstory.

## Character Poems
Write a poem about each character

## Character Obituaries
All three characters have died, write their obituary.

## Next Generation
Each character had a child who's going to pick up their story. Describe in two sentences how it starts.

## Lunchtime Scene
We're a quarter of the way through the character's story and it's lunchtime. How does the meal go?

## Late Night Phone Call
The three characters are trying to go to bed, then the phone rings.

## Demigod Creator
Come up with five demigod characters. Give them names and a two sentence backstory and describe their followers. Ask me if you should continue after each one.

## Animal Characters
Come up with ten animal characters. Give them names, species, genders and a two sentence backstory

## Heroes and Villains
Pick three of those characters, decide if they're a protagonist or an antagonist, and then create their opposing character with a two sentence backstory.

## Alternate Futures
Jesse has two kids in grade school. Describe two alternate futures for each of them. One for the event where their school suddenly closes, vs if it obtains funding and remains open.

## Decision Tree
Jesse is faced with a series of three challenging decisions. One at a time, tell me what that decision is, then give me two options to pick from. Use my selection to determine the next decision to ask me about. At the end, tell me how it ends for him.

## Three Questions Game
Ask me three questions, each has two possible answers. Use my answer as an input to the next question.
