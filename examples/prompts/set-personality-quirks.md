# Set Personality Quirks — Prompts

## What's your name?
Who are you? Tell me a bit about yourself.

## Weather report
What's the weather like in Seattle right now?

## Count to five
Can you count from 1 to 5 for me?

## Top 3 programming languages
List your top 3 programming languages and why you like them.

## Quick combo
What's your name, what's the weather, and count to 3 — all in one answer.
